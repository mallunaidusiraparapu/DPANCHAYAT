DIGITAL PANCHAYAT :

The Commissioner Panchayati Raj and Rural Development has initiated Digital Panchayat ,a Flagship G2C project, for Computerization and online issuance of citizen centric certificates. After a detailed system study by NIC, the department prioritized the applications to be automated for gram Panchayats. The application areas are Birth and Death Registrations, House Tax Demand Collections, Trade Licenses, Property valuation, mutations, water tap connection, NOC, Building permission, Layout permissions and MNREGA Job Card.
